 Stay Safe Online — Checklist (For College Students in India)

 General Safety Tips

 Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

 Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages

 Verify the authenticity of websites before entering login details (check HTTPS and URL spelling)

 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making transactions

 Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities

 Limit the personal information you share on social media

 Financial / UPI Safety

 Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank

 Always verify payment requests before approving; scammers may send “collect requests” instead of payments

 Use only trusted apps like Google Pay or PhonePe downloaded from official stores

 If Something Goes Wrong

 Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal

 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
